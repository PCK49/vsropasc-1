Rock, Paper, Scissors!

How Do You Play?

Choose Rock 🪨, Paper 📄, or Scissors ✂️ by clicking one of the buttons. The computer will also make a random choice. The rules are simple: 🪨 Rock beats ✂️ Scissors, 📄 Paper beats 🪨 Rock, and ✂️ Scissors beats 📄 Paper. If both you and the computer choose the same, it’s a tie! Try to win as many rounds as you can!
